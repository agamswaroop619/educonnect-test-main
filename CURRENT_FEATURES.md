# Current Features — Scholarly

Organised by who benefits from each feature.

---

## For Students

### Academics
- View subject-wise progress — chapter completion, quiz count, and DPP count per subject
- Track homework — see all assignments with due dates, submission status, and grades
- Download attached resources for each assignment
- Submit leave requests with date range and reason
- View and track leave status — approved, pending, rejected

### Performance & Reporting
- See overall CGPA and term-wise CGPA trend
- View class rank out of total students
- See subject-wise marks, grades, and teacher remarks per subject
- Read teacher's written strengths and growth-area feedback

### Attendance
- See overall attendance percentage for the year
- Track current attendance streak (consecutive days present)
- View monthly attendance trend across 6 months
- See per-day attendance log with present / absent / leave status

### Schedule & Planning
- View full weekly timetable — colour-coded by subject
- See upcoming school events, exams, and holidays in calendar view
- See today's pending and overdue tasks on the home dashboard

### Communication
- Read messages from teachers and school administration
- Reply to messages in a conversation thread
- Read official school circulars and announcements
- See pinned circulars separately for urgent notices

### Finance
- View annual fee structure broken down by category
- Track how much has been paid vs how much is due
- See upcoming payment due date
- View full transaction history with reference IDs and payment methods

### School Life
- View all achievements, awards, and recognitions
- Browse school event photo albums in the gallery
- Track library books currently issued and their due dates
- See overdue books and associated fines
- View reading history

### Transport
- See live bus route with current bus position
- View ETA to school
- Check route stops with passed/upcoming status
- Access driver contact number directly

### AI Assistant
- Ask questions about homework, concepts, or formulas
- Use quick prompts to explain a concept, break down a problem, or get practice recommendations

---

## For Teachers

### Class Management
- View full class roster with roll numbers and student IDs
- Filter students by performance status — Excellent, Good, Average, Needs Attention
- Search students by name or roll number
- See each student's attendance percentage with visual indicator
- See each student's CGPA and performance trend (improving / declining / stable)
- Identify at-risk students (those flagged as "Needs Attention")
- See top-performing student highlighted

### Subject Overview
- View class average score per subject
- See how many periods per week each subject has
- See performance classification (Excellent / Good / Average) per subject

### Communication
- Send and receive messages with students and parents
- Post circulars and announcements

---

## For Parents

### Child's Progress
- View child's overall CGPA and class rank
- See subject-wise marks and teacher remarks
- Track term-wise CGPA trend to see improvement over time

### Attendance Monitoring
- Check child's attendance percentage and recent attendance log
- See whether absences are marked as absent or leave

### Homework & Tasks
- See all assigned homework with due dates and submission status
- Know which assignments are pending or overdue

### Finance
- View annual fee breakdown
- Know exact amount paid and amount still due
- Check the next payment due date
- Access full payment history for records

### Communication
- Receive and reply to messages from class teacher and subject teachers
- Stay updated via school circulars and announcements

### Transport
- Know the bus route and real-time ETA
- Have the driver's contact number on hand

---

## For School Admins

### School-wide Communication
- Post circulars and announcements to all students and parents
- Pin important notices for visibility

### Class Overview
- Get a summary of class performance — average CGPA, average attendance, students needing attention
- Drill into individual student data across academics and attendance

### Events & Calendar
- Manage and display upcoming holidays, exams, events, and PTMs in the school calendar

---

## Cross-Role

- **Role-based access** — Teachers, parents, and admins each see a tailored view of the same data
- **Notifications** — Configurable alerts for attendance, homework, fees, circulars, and transport delays
- **Profile** — Student identity details and guardian information in one place
